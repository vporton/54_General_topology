product of uniform spaces


Definition.

Let {Xα}α∈I be a nonempty family of uniform spaces. The is the weakest uniformity (http://planetmath.org/UniformitiesOnASetFormACompleteLattice) on the Cartesian product X=∏α∈IXα making all the projection maps πα:X→Xα uniformly continuous.

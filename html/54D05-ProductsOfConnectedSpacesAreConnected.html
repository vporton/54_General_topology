products of connected spaces are connected


Theorem 1

[1, 2] Let (Xi)i∈I be a family of topological spaces. Then the product space

∏i∈IXi

with the product topology is connected if and only if each space Xi is connected.

As is true of most results in topology involving products, the forward implication requires the axiom of choice.
